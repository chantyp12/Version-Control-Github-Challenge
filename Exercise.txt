Explain what the terminal is in your own words.

The terminal is the system on your computer that allows you to make changes on your OS and open/create files.

What is the major difference between Git and Github?

Git is the system that you download onto your terminal that allows you to manage/edit your code. 
The GitHub is the cloud (Hub) platform that allows you to manage the different code projects you have as you can also open it up to others.

Why do we use Github?

It is easy to collaborate with other engineers and store code projects that we work on.
